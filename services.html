What is a Power Purchase Agreement?

A Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) is a financial arrangement in which DAZ Solar Partners installs, owns, and operates a solar energy system on your property. You agree to purchase the electricity generated by the system at a predetermined rate — typically lower than what you currently pay your utility.

Key Benefits of a PPA

  • No capital expenditure: We fund the entire project. You pay nothing upfront.
  • Immediate savings: Your electricity rate is locked in below current utility pricing from day one.
  • No maintenance burden: We own the system and are responsible for all monitoring, maintenance, and repairs.
  • Predictable costs: Your energy rate is fixed or escalates at a known, agreed-upon percentage — far below typical utility increases.
  • Environmental impact: Reduce your carbon footprint and demonstrate sustainability leadership to customers and stakeholders.
  • Property value: Solar installations can increase the value and attractiveness of commercial properties.

Who Qualifies?

Our PPA solution is designed for commercial and industrial properties with significant electricity consumption. Ideal candidates include warehouses, office buildings, retail centers, manufacturing facilities, and multi-tenant properties.

Frequently Asked Questions

PPA terms typically range from 10 to 25 years. The specific term depends on the size of the system, your energy needs, and the rate structure that works best for your business.

The PPA can typically be transferred to the new property owner or tenant. We work with you to ensure a smooth transition. In some cases, early buyout options are also available.

We work with qualified installers who use methods that preserve your roof warranty. In many cases, solar panels actually protect the underlying roof from weather, extending its lifespan.

Your property remains connected to the utility grid. When the solar system isn't producing enough electricity (cloudy days, nighttime), you seamlessly draw from the grid as you normally would. You only pay us for the solar electricity actually produced.

Savings vary by location, energy usage, and utility rates, but most commercial customers see 10-30% reduction in electricity costs from day one. Savings increase over time as utility rates rise while your PPA rate stays predictable.
