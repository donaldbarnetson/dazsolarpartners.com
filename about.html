About Us

Our mission, values, and commitment to clean energy

Our Mission

DAZ Solar Partners is committed to making solar energy accessible and financially compelling for commercial and industrial property owners. We believe that the transition to clean energy should not require a large capital outlay or operational burden — and our Power Purchase Agreement model proves it.

What Drives Us

Rising electricity costs are one of the largest controllable expenses for property owners and businesses. We founded DAZ Solar Partners to address this problem head-on: provide immediate savings through solar energy with zero upfront investment.

Our approach is simple. We invest in the solar infrastructure on your property so you don't have to. You benefit from cleaner, cheaper electricity while we handle everything from installation to ongoing maintenance.

Our Values

  • Transparency: Clear terms, predictable rates, and no hidden costs. You always know exactly what you're paying and what you're saving.
  • Partnership: We succeed when our partners succeed. Our business model is aligned with delivering long-term value to property owners.
  • Reliability: We stand behind our systems with full maintenance and performance monitoring for the life of the agreement.
  • Sustainability: Every kilowatt-hour of solar energy we deliver displaces fossil fuel generation, contributing to a cleaner environment for future generations.

Who We Serve

We work with commercial and industrial property owners who are looking to reduce operating expenses and increase net operating income. Our ideal partners include:

  • Commercial real estate owners and operators
  • Industrial and manufacturing facilities
  • Retail centers and shopping plazas
  • Warehouses and distribution centers
  • Office parks and multi-tenant buildings
  • Agricultural operations
